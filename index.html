About Me

As a former UFC fighter, I've had the incredible opportunity to travel the world. I've competed in countries such as Canada, Sweden, and USA, just to name a few. It's been an incredible experience to explore different cultures, try new foods, and meet people from all walks of life. Traveling for martial arts has taught me to appreciate the beauty of diversity and has given me a unique perspective on life. I'm grateful for every opportunity I've had to travel and look forward to continuing to explore the world in the future with my girlfriend and kids.

- Pablo The Scarecrow Garza
